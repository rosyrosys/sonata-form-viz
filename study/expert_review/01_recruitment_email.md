## English version (use if recruiting non-Korean-speaking expert)

**Subject:** Mozart K. 545 sonata-form visualization tool — 1-hour expert review request

Dear Professor [Name],

I am [Name, affiliation] preparing a manuscript for the TISMIR
(Transactions of the International Society for Music Information
Retrieval) Tools-and-Datasets track. The submission presents an
open-source web tool that synchronizes the notated score, audio
playback, and a two-level formal-analysis layer for Mozart's Piano
Sonata K. 545, Movement I, with particular pedagogical focus on
the work's celebrated subdominant recapitulation.

To strengthen the manuscript with a preliminary expert validation
ahead of the planned randomized controlled trial, I am inviting
two domain experts to participate in a 1-hour evaluation session.
Your background in [specific expertise: e.g., "classical form
pedagogy" / "undergraduate music theory teaching"] would be
particularly valuable for assessing the tool.

**Session structure** (1 hour total):
1. Self-exploration of the tool (10 min).
2. Walk-through of the 30-minute classroom lesson plan, acting as
   the teacher (30 min).
3. System Usability Scale (Brooke 1996) — 10 items (5 min).
4. Semi-structured interview on pedagogical fidelity, affordance
   value, and risk of student misconceptions (15 min).

**Logistics:**
- Online (Zoom / Google Meet) or in person, at your convenience.
- No preparation required — the tool runs in any browser.
- Findings will be reported anonymously in §6 of the manuscript
  (e.g., "Expert A / Expert B"); with explicit consent, named
  acknowledgment will be added to the manuscript.

**Compensation:** [State honorarium if available, or note "as this
work is unfunded, I am unable to offer an honorarium and would be
deeply grateful for your time"]. In return I will share the
published manuscript and the Zenodo archive link, and would be
honoured to invite your continued advisory involvement in the
planned follow-up RCT.

The tool is available for preview at
https://rosyrosys.github.io/sonata-form-viz/. If you would be
willing to participate, please reply and I will send a participant
information sheet and propose a time.

With thanks for your consideration,
