## §D. System Usability Scale (~ 5 min)

> This section uses Brooke's (1996) *System Usability Scale* (10 items,
> 5-point Likert). For Korean-language administration, the validated
> Korean version (Kim, Park, & Lee, 2014, *Journal of the HCI Society
> of Korea*, 9(2), 31–40) is used.
>
> **Out of respect for the original instrument, the 10 items are not
> reproduced in this Markdown file.** In the actual session, the
> published instrument is attached as an IRB appendix and used
> verbatim.

Scoring (for reference):
1. Odd-numbered items: (response − 1)
2. Even-numbered items: (5 − response)
3. Sum × 2.5 = SUS total (0–100)

Interpretation (Sauro & Lewis, 2016):
- ≥ 80.3 = A (Excellent)
- 68 = industry mean
- < 51.7 = F
